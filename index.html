I am a UX/ UI designer and artist based in Brooklyn, NY. I love making things beautiful. My love of informed design stems from my experiences in digital marketing. Good design coupled with engaging content makes THE difference when running a digital marketing campaign of any kind. Happy, informed customers lead to marketers hitting business goals and objectives.


I have a diverse background with my B.S. in Biochemistry and experience working in multiple channels of digital marketing. Currently, I'm sharpening my design and writing skills at a marketing agency in Manhattan and freelancing. In my free time, I explore different painting techniques - check out some of my illustrations! Many prints of my work are also available on my Etsy shop.


Interested in hiring me? Feel free to get in touch and download my resume. I am also a UserFeel certified tester.
